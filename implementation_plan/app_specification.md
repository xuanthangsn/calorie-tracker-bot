## what is this app about
    - personal assistant that help you track your daily calorie intake
    - you can chat with it to log calorie intake for a meal, modify last meal log. You can ask it to report your calorie intake of specific day, week, month,...
    - it will refuse to answer if your question, or request is irrelevant
    - this app is designed as a ReAct agent (reasonning and act agent)
